You can:

- Consult any center gong plan and/or timetables with the CONSULT PLANNING link here below. 

- See the detailed status and configuration of a specific center by selecting it and then click STATUS AND CONFIGURATION

- Modify the plan and/or timetables of a specific center for which you are a registered planner by selecting it and then click MODIFY GONG PLANNING.
You can only modify a center gong planning if the center is not currently being modified or in the process of being sent to the center: it has status 'free' on its STATUS AND CONFIGURATION page. If the center is not 'free', you will be redirected to the STATUS AND CONFIGURATION page for this center.
