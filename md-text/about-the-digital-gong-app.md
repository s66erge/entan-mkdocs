This web app is used to manage gong planning for Vipassana dhamma centers. It is the companion app of the minicomputer (Rasperry Pi) installed in these centers to drive the audio gong system(s), even when their Internet connection is down.

You can find more information on this Vipassana wiki page, in the section "Digital Gong":
<a href="https://wiki.dhamma.org/display/GONGS/Electronic+Gongs+Home" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Electronic Gongs Home</a>
For more info, including how it could possibly be installed in your center(s), contact (one of) the authors indicated on the wiki page.
