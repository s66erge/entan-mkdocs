- The gongs table list the different gongs sounds and duration available in this center. They were configured during the system installation in this center. 
- The targets table list the different connections between the network of local minicomputers and the sound amplifiers driving one or more gong loudspeakers.  The 'longname' describes their purpose/location. If there is only one target, there is only one minicomputer and all gong loudspeakers of the center play the same gong sound everywhere.
