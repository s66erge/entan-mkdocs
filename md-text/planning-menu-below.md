- **(re)Start getting plans**: (re)loads the current center gong plan and the dhamma.org current plan, combines them and shows if there are issues for the gong planning. If you loaded it alredy and you made changes, they will be lost.
- **Load saved plan**: shows the plan with the changes you already made since the last '(re)Start getting plans'. Allows you to work on the timetables and then continue working on the plan where you left it. 
- **(re)Start timetables**: (re)loads the current center gong timetables and checks that it fits the gong planning constraints. If you loaded them alredy and you made changes, they will be lost.
- **Load saved timetables**: shows the timetables with the changes you already made since the last '(re)Start getting plans'. Allows you to return working on the plan and then continue working on the timetables where you left them.
- **return NO CHANGES** and **SAVE ALL CHANGES**: see above help text **How to save your work**
- **Download PDF**: downloads a pdf copy of this entire page. Remember to close down help text(s) before. Suggestion: format your pdf with landscape, max. 80% scale, minimum margins and background graphics
- **open this center status tab**: opens a new tab to see this center full status page. **Then come back here to complete or abandon your work**.
- **open all centers consult tab**: opens a new tab with the plans/timetebles of other center. **Then come back here to complete or abandon your work**.
